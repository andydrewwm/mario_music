Super Mario World

Super Mario World was the first game in the series to introduce Mario's iconic companion Yoshi! Being the mighty dinosaur he is, Yoshi lets Mario ride on his back as he marches onward to rescue Princess Peach.

Riding on Yoshi changes how the player will interact with their environment. Whether they choose to play more risky or cautiously is up to them. Regardless, they will approach a level differently than they would.

What I find brilliant about Super Mario World's music is its ability to convey a difference in gameplay through its music. Why explain it to you when I can SHOW you what I mean! Try clicking on Mario or Yoshi in the figure below (make sure your sound is on)!

When you click on either character, the muusic that plays is what a player would hear in game when they are playing with or without Yoshi. Did you try clicking Mario, and then Yoshi? Or Yoshi first, and then Mario? You should be able to hear extra drums playing in the music whenever Yoshi is chosen, and less when it is just Mario!

Since different level types have different music, there are more variations of musical effect. I have included more examples of how the music will change with and without Yoshi for your enjoyment, so click away!

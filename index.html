About

I made this website while taking PAT 305 (Video Game Music) at the University of Michigan. This course inspired me to go back and listen closely to the soundtracks of my favorite Mario games.

Thus, my goal with this website is to present the audio effects used in Mario games that make their sound so fun and interactive. I hope you enjoy!

By Andrew Wilks-Millard
